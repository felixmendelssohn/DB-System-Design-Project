AMAZON-LIKE ONLINE STORE DATABASE

THE ENTITIES OF INTEREST include :-
1 Addresses.
2 Customers.
3 Deliveries
4 Orders.
5 Payments
6 Products.
7 Suppliers.

The Busines Rules are as follows :-
1 A Customer can have zero,one or many ORDERS.
2 An ADDRESS can be associated with only one ADDRESS.
3 An ADDRESS can be associated with only one CUSTOMER.
4 A DELIVERY is associated with just one ORDER.
5 An ORDER must be associated with one and only one CUSTOMER.
6 An ORDER can be associated with one or many PRODUCTS.
7 A PAYMENT must be associated with one and only one ORDER.
